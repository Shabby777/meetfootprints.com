I am a therapist who provides a thoughtful, client-centered approach to treatment. I support individuals, couples and families in managing anxiety, grief, trauma, and life issues. My work focuses on helping clients build emotional awareness, strengthen coping skills, and create meaningful, sustainable change. I believe therapy should be a safe, supportive space where you feel seen, heard, and understood. I work collaboratively with clients to explore emotions, heal from past experiences, and develop practical tools for navigating life’s challenges. I have experience working with NYC high school students and families for 15+ years by providing support and guidance. I look forward to gaining a healthy dynamic between us by being supportive and empathetic. I am open to working with clients from all communities. Looking forward to talking with you!
